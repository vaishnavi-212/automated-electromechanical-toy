

##  Problem Statement

To design and develop a durable, interactive, and user-friendly electromechanical toy that enhances entertainment for children across a wide age group (3–16 years). The system should incorporate remote control functionality and communication capabilities to enable responsive and engaging interactions based on user input.

The toy must ensure safety, reliability, and ease of use by utilizing non-toxic and eco-friendly materials while maintaining low power consumption. It should support mobility and may include light and sound features to enhance user experience.

Additionally, the design must be cost-effective (under ₹5000), lightweight (less than 1 kg), and compact (10–30 cm in size). It should be robust enough to withstand minor impacts and ensure a minimum operational lifespan of one year. The system should also allow for future upgrades and be completed within a development timeline of four months.
