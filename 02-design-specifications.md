##  Design Specifications
###  Objectives
- User-friendly, engaging, and safe design  
- Ability to withstand minor crashes and falls  
- Use of child-friendly colors  
- Designed purely for entertainment  
- No manual or tutorial required  

###  Constraints
- Target age group: 3–16 years  
- Cost below ₹5000  
- Compact size (10–30 cm)  
- Weight less than 1 kg  
- Easy to use (difficulty level: 2/5)  
- Lifespan: 1–3 years  
- Development time: ~4 months  

###  Functions
- Use of non-toxic and eco-friendly materials  
- Provide light and sound features  
- Enable app/remote-controlled functionality  
- Operate using suitable power source  
- Provide basic mobility and interaction
